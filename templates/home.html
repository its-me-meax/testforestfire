🔥 FFMC - Fine Fuel Moisture Code

Represents moisture content of litter and fine fuels. Higher values indicate drier conditions and increased fire risk.

🌿 DMC - Duff Moisture Code

Indicates moisture content of loosely compacted organic layers. Important for fire intensity and spread.

⚡ ISI - Initial Spread Index

Combines wind speed and FFMC to indicate expected rate of fire spread. Higher values mean faster spread.

🎯 Fire Weather Index

Comprehensive measure combining all factors to assess overall fire danger. Used by firefighters and forest managers.
